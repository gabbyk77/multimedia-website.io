TYLER THE CREATOR



About

Tyler the Creator is an American rapper and singer who released his first album in 2008, and his most recent one in 2021. He has won multiple awards, such as a Grammy for his 2019 album Igor. He was originally part of the rap group Odd Future from 2007-2015. The group included many other artists, such as Frank Ocean, Earl Sweatshirt, and more. Tyler helped desing the group's merch, including stickers, skateboards, and clothing. On his own, Tyler has released 7 albums, made his own merch and clothing brand, and is still making music now.
